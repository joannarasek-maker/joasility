Turkey Spaghetti

Nutrition values are approximate and based on 1 lb ground turkey, pasta divided into 4 servings, tomato sauce, olive oil, and parmesan.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 jar tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Basil
  • Pasta
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Dice the onion and garlic.
  2. Heat a little olive oil in a large pan.
  3. Add the onion and garlic and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add the ground turkey and cook until lightly browned.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and basil.
  6. Add the tomato sauce and stir well.
  7. Simmer for about 20 minutes, until the sauce becomes rich and flavorful.
  8. Serve with cooked pasta.
  9. Finish with parmesan cheese.
