Millet Breakfast Bowl

A nourishing and satisfying breakfast made with millet, high-protein fat-free skyr, blueberries, walnuts, and dried cranberries. Millet provides complex carbohydrates and important minerals, skyr adds a generous amount of protein with no fat, and blueberries contribute fiber and antioxidants. A simple breakfast that is both filling and nutrient-dense.

1 serving About 430 kcal About 26 g protein

Millet Breakfast Bowl

Nutrition values are approximate and based on 50 g dry millet, 100 g skyr, 100 g blueberries, 10 g dried cranberries, and 10 g walnuts.

Ingredients

  • 50 g millet
  • About 125 ml water
  • 10 g dried cranberries
  • 10 g walnuts
  • 100 g skyr (high-protein, fat-free)
  • 100 g blueberries
  • Optional: 1–2 tablespoons erythritol

Instructions

  1. Rinse the millet thoroughly before cooking.
  2. Add the millet and water to a small saucepan.
  3. Cook over low heat for 15–20 minutes until the millet becomes soft and absorbs the water.
  4. Near the end of cooking, add the dried cranberries and walnuts.
  5. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  6. Stir in the skyr.
  7. Add the blueberries and gently mix everything together.
  8. If you prefer a sweeter taste, add 1–2 tablespoons of erythritol.
