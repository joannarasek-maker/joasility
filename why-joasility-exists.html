Why Joasility?

Jun 6, 2026 4 min read
Reflection Travel Journal Life in Motion

Joasility is my travel journal.

I share moments I have captured in photos, videos and notes. This is not a travel guide and it is not a how-to website. It is simply a part of my life.

Over the years I have visited many places, met interesting people and spent a lot of time in nature. I don't want those memories to end up forgotten on a hard drive somewhere. They are stories of people, places and wildlife. I want them to keep living every time someone looks at them.

The physical activity you see here is my way of staying healthy and capable for as long as possible.

I want to keep hiking in the mountains, riding my bike, swimming, diving and exploring new places on my own. For me, fitness is not the goal. The goal is to have a healthy and functional body that allows me to continue the journey.

Food is part of that too.

The recipes I share are simple meals that fit my lifestyle. Nothing fancy. Just quick, nourishing food that helps me stay active and feel good.

I know not everyone will like everything they see here.

I also know many things could be done better. The photos could be better. The videos could be better. The writing could be better.

But that's okay.

I have spent enough time chasing perfection. In my case, perfectionism never really helped. More often it stopped me from doing things because they never felt good enough.

So now I focus on doing things well enough and doing them consistently.

I don't think happiness is waiting at the finish line. Goals are useful because they give us direction, but what matters most is enjoying the process and having fun along the way.

This is simply my perspective.

Everyone has their own, and that is a good thing.

Thanks for being here.
